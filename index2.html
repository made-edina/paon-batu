Our Story

Three best friends, a journey of passion. What defines the perfect Babi Guling?

It was a long search. Day and night, from the most famous places in the city to secluded spots in the mountains, there was just something not quite right.

Even with the cooking skill and the fresh ingredients to support it, the ambience just didn't fit the situation.

An artist's muse is their greatest strength, and after a long night of contemplation, the artist among us suggested the beautiful landscape of Paon Batu. By opening Paon Batu, we have decided to crown Babi Guling as a noble dish and opened Paon Batu. A gorgeous setting; the warmth of home; the best of drinks; and last but not least, delicious Babi Guling.

For those unable to eat pork, we have also made the option of Ayam Panggang available.​

All food lovers welcome!
